Extracts the rotary encoder absolute position as function of time from fronts detected on the 2 channels. Outputs in units of radius parameters, by default radians Coding options detailed here: http://www.ni.com/tutorial/7109/pt/ Here output is clockwise from subject perspective

Parameters

  • ta â time of fronts on channel A

  • pa â polarity of fronts on channel A

  • tb â time of fronts on channel B

  • pb â polarity of fronts on channel B

  • ticks â number of ticks corresponding to a full revolution (1024 for IBL rotary encoder)

  • radius â radius of the wheel. Defaults to 1 for an output in radians

  • coding â x1, x2 or x4 coding (IBL default is x4)

Returns

indices vector (ta) and position vector
